Privacy Policy

Controller: Orthodox Companion / rg-ru OC-project. This is a public prototype hosted on GitHub Pages. Replace this line with the full legal name, postal address, and contact email of the operator before a commercial or public launch beyond this prototype.

Purpose: The site provides an Orthodox Christian companion experience: fasting calendar, readings, study progress, prayer wall preview, church finder, and source-backed assistant prototype.

Data processed in this web release: app settings, consent choices, theme, jurisdiction, bookmarks, local reading progress, local notes, local prayer preview entries, local chat history, and optional browser geolocation for church sorting.

Location: Location is requested only after the user clicks Find Near Me, accepts the in-app location notice, and grants the browser permission. Coordinates are used once in the browser to sort sample churches by distance. Coordinates are not stored, not sent to a backend, and not shared by this static site.

Legal basis: essential local storage is used to provide features requested by the user. Optional location is based on consent. Future analytics, marketing, community accounts, Firebase sync, or AI backend processing must remain off until a specific consent and production privacy notice are in place.

Storage period: local app data remains in the user's browser until the user clears browser storage or uses the controls in this app. Consent choices are remembered for 180 days unless changed earlier.

User rights: where GDPR applies, users may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and withdrawal of consent. For this static prototype, most data is stored only in the user's own browser and can be deleted locally.

Important: Prayer requests, assistant messages, notes, and religious preferences can be sensitive. The production app must add stronger account security, moderation, clergy/source review, export/deletion flows, and a reviewed legal notice before collecting this data on a server.

Cookie and Local Storage Policy

This site uses first-party cookies and browser local storage. It does not use advertising cookies, third-party analytics, or cross-site tracking in this release.

Necessary Consent record, app shell, PWA cache, and feature state needed to deliver user-requested functions.
Preferences Theme, jurisdiction, progress, bookmarks, notes, and local-only personalization.
Location A consent flag for the Find Near Me feature. Exact coordinates are not persisted.
Analytics Reserved for a future privacy-preserving analytics setup. Disabled now.

Optional choices can be changed at any time through Cookie Settings. Essential storage cannot be disabled because the site needs it to remember the consent decision and provide requested app functions.

Terms of Use

Orthodox Companion is an educational and devotional software prototype. It is not a substitute for pastoral guidance, confession, medical advice, legal advice, emergency services, or direct communication with a parish.

Calendar, fasting, readings, and church data may vary by jurisdiction and parish. Users should confirm fasting rules, services, pastoral questions, and sacramental matters with their priest or parish.

The assistant prototype must answer only from approved Orthodox sources in production and should refuse unsupported claims. Its current responses are illustrative and not authoritative.

Users must not submit unlawful, abusive, defamatory, private, or sensitive information about others. Community prayer features require moderation before production launch.
